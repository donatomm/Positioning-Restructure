|     This document summarizes a discussion with Georgiana Lowy, an expert in customer experience mapping and author of \"Forget the Funnel.\" Lowy argues against the traditional sales funnel, deeming it lazy and ineffective for understanding the customer\'s full journey and optimizing their experience. Instead, she advocates for \"customer-led growth,\" which involves deeply understanding customers, their relationship with a product from pre-awareness to expansion, and operationalizing this understanding to improve outcomes. Her methodology, often leveraging \"Jobs to Be Done\" (JTBD) research, helps companies identify critical moments of value, focus on high-LTV customers, and develop resonant messaging. She also discusses the limitations of AI in foundational customer understanding and shares a case study where shifting messaging based on customer research led to a 90% increase in sign-ups and a 40% increase in trial-to-paid conversion. |

| 3.  Why \"Forget the Funnel\"?                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - Lowy believes traditional funnels are \"lazy and ineffective\" because they flatten the view of the customer, offering generic models that don\'t account for individual customer needs.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - Funnels omit the crucial \"before\" and \"after\" stages of the customer journey, neglecting the initial problem recognition and the post-conversion experience, especially for recurring revenue businesses.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - They are not actionable for optimizing customer experience and increasing conversions.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| 4. |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - The alternative is \"customer-led growth,\" a process focused on deeply understanding customers and their relationship with the product.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - This involves mapping the customer\'s journey from before they know the product exists, through to expanded and sophisticated usage, and even referrals.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - The goal is to identify and optimize \"moments of value\" and \"milestones\" in the customer relationship, measuring success through relevant KPIs.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |

| 6.  The Problem with Zero-Touch & PLG Models                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - Zero-touch and product-led growth (PLG) companies often lack sufficient customer research, leading to guesswork in positioning and scaling.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - Without salespeople, these companies can miss crucial market shifts and competitor emergence, only noticing issues when numbers decline.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - Scaling effectively requires a deep understanding of the problem being solved, the product\'s value, and the optimal way to introduce it to customers.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |

| 7.  Using Jobs to Be Done (JTBD)                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - Lowy\'s methodology leverages \"Jobs to Be Done\" research, typically involving 10-12 targeted customer conversations.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - This research consistently reveals multiple \"jobs to be done\" that customers \"hire\" a product for, even when companies initially perceive their customer base as homogeneous.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - Identifying these distinct jobs allows companies to focus on higher-LTV customers, leading to improved acquisition, retention, and willingness to pay.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - The process helps identify critical moments of value in the customer journey and establish KPIs that measure customer value delivery, rather than just business metrics.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |

|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - While some teams readily embrace focusing on specific customer segments, others may resist, fearing a shrinking market or missing opportunities.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |

|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - Lowy expresses caution about relying solely on AI for foundational customer understanding, especially for high-stakes decisions like messaging strategy.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |

|     - AI can be a valuable tool *after* a human has established a foundational understanding through deep customer research, such as JTBD.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |

| 11. A Surprising Case Study: Focusing on the *Right* Customer                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - Lowy recounts a case study where a company with excellent content and traffic had flat revenue for a year because they were focused on the \"wrong customer\" or too many customers.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - A vocal group of \"poor fit customers\" were consuming resources, while high-LTV customers were flying under the radar.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                           |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - By updating just three website pages (homepage, features, product) to focus messaging on the higher-quality customer, sign-ups increased by nearly 90%, and the trial-to-paid conversion rate increased by 40%.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     - This highlights how easy it is for companies to misdirect resources and the significant impact of deeply understanding and targeting the right customer.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
