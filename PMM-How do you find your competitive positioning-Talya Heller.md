[How do you find your competitive positioning?]{.mark}

[I say "find" because you don't really "make it"]{.mark}

[It's more like you uncover it 🕵️‍♀️]{.mark}

[Talya Heller](https://www.linkedin.com/in/talyahellermba18/) [breaks it down into 5 steps:]{.mark}

[1️⃣ Know your audience]{.mark}

[Create a rich ideal customer profile with specific firmographics, pains, and triggers. It should also clarify how they're solving their pains now, who they're looking up to, and what communities or sources they trust.]{.mark}

[This comes from things like:]{.mark}

[↳ customer interviews]{.mark}

[↳ customer-facing teams]{.mark}

[↳ CRM data like expansions, sales velocity, and win rates by use case, vertical, tier, and product]{.mark}

[2️⃣ Know the market (ie: other alternatives)]{.mark}

[Features are easy to imitate. Instead, focus on expertise, vertical knowledge, distribution channels, brand strength, and thought leadership. These will tell you their GTM strategy and make it easier to predict where they're headed.]{.mark}

[Ask the real questions:]{.mark}

[↳ Who\'s their ideal customer profile?]{.mark}

[↳ Who\'s the villain in their story?]{.mark}

[↳ What channels are they using?]{.mark}

[↳ Where are they investing?]{.mark}

[3️⃣ Know where to focus]{.mark}

[Hone in on who your competitors are targeting. Who they say they\'re talking to (featured logos, use cases, content partnerships) and who\'s actually purchasing (filter review sites by firmographics, monitor social media).]{.mark}

[Look for the gaps --- that's your opportunity!]{.mark}

[4️⃣ Know your unique winning factors (a.k.a. differentiators)]{.mark}

[When you know your ICP, you also know why you win. Break down the distinct capabilities you provide, but also the benefits they deliver, pains they solve, and how it manifests in your product.]{.mark}

[Example: \"Scale\" on its own means nothing. But if your ICP struggles to get company-wide adoption, and your product integrates with tools they already use -- now you\'re telling a different story:]{.mark}

[5️⃣ Competitive Positioning Mapping]{.mark}

[Each distinct capability becomes a potential differentiator. Compare each capability against the alternatives your ICP actually considers. Any capability that competitors can\'t match gives you edge. Add them up and you've got a clear roadmap to win.]{.mark}

[So, to recap you find your competitive positioning by:]{.mark}

[1️⃣ Knowing your audience]{.mark}

[2️⃣ Knowing the market]{.mark}

[3️⃣ Knowing where to focus]{.mark}

[4️⃣ Knowing your unique winning factors]{.mark}

[5️⃣ Mapping your competitive positioning]{.mark}
