|     Stratton describes her journey as a writer, starting in brand messaging for Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG) in Europe, where she learned to connect with consumers using few words. A move to the US led her to a B2B marketing agency, where she initially found the technical nature of clients challenging. However, she realized that jargon-heavy language was a messaging problem, not a technical one. She began applying CPG principles to B2B tech, simplifying complex platforms into buyer-centric outcomes. In 2015/2016, she started her consultancy, focusing on messaging for B2B tech companies, and later began teaching her approach to marketing teams.Positioning vs. Messaging vs. Copywriting\ |
|     \                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
|     Stratton defines the three terms:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
|     - **Positioning:** Strategy that defines what a product is, who it\'s for, and its unique value.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
|     - **Messaging:** How positioning is articulated through a handful of messages, consistently communicating unique value to the buyer. It\'s part strategy, part writing.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
|     - **Copywriting:** Takes messaging and adapts it for different channels to drive specific actions (e.g., learn more, get a demo). It\'s primarily writing with a strategic element.\                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
|       She notes that in practice, these often blend, with messaging engagements sometimes involving positioning work and a touch of copywriting.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |

| 5.  The Headline and Tagline Obsession\                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
|     \                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
|     Dunford expresses frustration with the obsession over taglines and headlines in positioning workshops, often leading to vague, three-word phrases like \"accelerate, innovate, procreate.\" Stratton agrees, likening it to the \"curse of knowledge,\" where marketers, close to their product, imbue these words with meaning that outsiders don\'t grasp.How to Write Better Headlines\                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
|     \                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
|     Stratton offers simple techniques for writing effective headlines:                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                 |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
|     - **Prompts:** Use \"Imagine if you could Blank\" to create aspirational headlines, especially for technical products, tapping into future states and emotions.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
|     - **Formulas and tricks:** Headlines need to grab attention and encourage further reading, as 80% of people read headlines but only 20% read body copy.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
|     - **Specificity and superpower:** Instead of vague statements, focus on specific details and the \"new superpower\" the product offers. For example, instead of \"accelerate your accounting,\" use \"close your books without ever having to open a spreadsheet.\" This provides a clear, memorable takeaway.\                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
|       Specificity in headlines is crucial for retaining differentiation and value.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
